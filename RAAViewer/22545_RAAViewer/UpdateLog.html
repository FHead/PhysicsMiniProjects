Basic information

Instructions

Here are the instructions on all the features:

  • All underlined fields can be clicked and selected
  • Choose the "X" and "Y" in the bottom right of the plot to choose whether to do log scale.
  • The color box in the left of each line can be clicked: they hide curves on the plot.
  • Mouse left-button drag chooses an area to zoom in.
  • Right click to zoom out. Or click the globe.
  • Click on the download button to download plot as pdf.
  • The location of the cross hair (when you hover in the plot) is shown next to the log scale toggles.
  • Dot is central value. Stat uncertainty is the short horizontal line on the vertical lines. End of vertical lines are total uncertainty (stat + sys + global).

Other information

Due to the large amount of data results from all the experiments, the data included in this RAA viewer is an evolving collection. Yi will add a couple curves when she finds time. If you have something you really want to compare, tell her so that they can be imported sooner. Not only RAB, but other observables (flow, hadron ratios, etc) can be requested
